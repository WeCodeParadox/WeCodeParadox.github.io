Vision

Integrated Intelligent Research (IIR)-India is a gateway for educational empowerment.

Mission

IIR become a model resource center to serve for higher education institutes in the field of Training and Development, Ranking and Accreditation, Research, Consultancy and placement.

About

Integrated Intelligent Research (IIR) is a gateway for Educational Empowerment established in the year 2012. IIR is providing solution for Journal Publications, Organizing Conferences, Placement Services, Outcome Based Education (OBE) implementation towards accreditations for Educational Quality Management System (E-QMS) which includes Training and Development, Research support,.
  • To service the higher educational institutes to achieve their mission and vision.
  • To support HEI to meet the national and international standards in their academic and administrative process.
  • To offer training to the students community for their integrated development.
  • To assist the academic community for their research and professional development.
  • To collaborate similar interest groups to promote scientific research project, events and Publications.
